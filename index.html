Etsy.
Custom-made happinnes.

Overview

Project in a snapshot

The brief

Create a feature that allows customers to post a custom-made product request, and receive quotes from multiple artisans.

Tools and methods used

    Competitive Analysis

    User Survey & Affinity Map

    User Interviews

    Personas & User Journeys

    Design Studio & Sketches

    User Testing

    Wireframing & Prototyping

The outcome

New custom service that allows buyers to post comprehensive briefs for custom-made products and the sellers to provide quotes for products they are interested in making.

UX Research

Get to know your users first and find out about their needs.

Competitve Analysis

We analysed a number of similar services with services like Folksy, Bespokee or Custom Made being main online competitors.We discovered that while both Etsy and thier competitors were alreday offerring custom-made service to their customers in some form the solution offered wasn't always the most staright-forward or user-friendly.





Survey & Affinity Map

The purpose of the survey was to provide input for the design process and gather quantitative data from participants. In order to better understand the research data we received, and capture insights and pain points mentioned by the participants we created an Affinity Map.






User Interviews

While the survey provided us with quantitave data, in order to further inform our design process we needed qualitative data and the answer as to why users were worried to buy or sell custom-made products online. We interviewed 5 users and the information gained during this process helped us to develop our personas.





Personas & user journeys

From the data and information gathered during the reserach we created our primary personas, i.e. representatives of particular audeince segments that we were targeting with the new product.

Design Process

Make your goals clear and defined and validate your ideas early on!

Design studio & sketches

We carried out design process by starting with Design studio, then moving onto sketches and then onto digital wireframes following user testing on paper prototype.

Usability testing

We tested our wireframes several times with users using testing software Silverback. The insights we gained from usability tetsing led to further iterations on wireframes until we felt the users can complete their tasks without any major issues.
