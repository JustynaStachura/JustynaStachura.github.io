About Justyna

Hi, my name is Justyna!

I am Justyna, a Product Designer based in London. My goal is to create products & services that people love using and keep coming back to.

I'm a bit of an all rounder working across UX research, UX & service design and UI. You are likely to find me talking to businesses and customers, mapping out user journeys, service blueprints, running workshops and working on wireframes and UIs.

I strongly believe that product success is based on really understanding what your customers need by bringing them on the journey from day one. The other ingredients for success are good team collaboration, partnership and last but not least, genuine passion for what you do and how you do it.

I enjoy talking to fellow designers and sharing the knowledge and passion for good design - not just on the web but in every area of life. After all design is everywhere.

When I am not designing, I am usually practising yoga, travelling or simply hanging around my local area of Crouch End.
